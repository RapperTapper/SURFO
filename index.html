Switzerland may be landlocked, but its vibrant river surfing scene defies the country’s Alpine borders. In the heart of Zurich, the River Limmat offers an extraordinary urban surfing spot beneath the quaint bridges, drawing both locals and tourists to its unique waves. This sport blends the thrill of surfing with the picturesque scenery of Swiss cities, making it a must-try adventure for every thrill-seeker.
